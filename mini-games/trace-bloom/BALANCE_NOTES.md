# Balance Notes

This version adjusts the trace-energy economy.

## Changes

- Passive trace regeneration was reduced heavily.
- Trace energy now regenerates slowly enough that green packets matter.
- Trace energy does **not** reset between waves.
- Completing a wave gives only a small +20 trace-energy boost.
- Green packets remain valuable and restore a large amount of trace energy.
- The intended strategy is now to route between cyan nodes and green packets.

## Intended loop

1. Hold **Space** or **Left Mouse** near cyan nodes to bloom them.
2. Watch your TRACE bar.
3. Collect green packets when trace energy gets low.
4. Clear all nodes to advance to the next wave.
5. You only get a small trace boost between waves, not a full refill.
